TRAINING TIPS

The fundamentals never go out of style. Master these basics and everything else falls into place.

  • 🌙
    Sleep is your biggest gains toolMuscles grow during rest, not during workouts. Aim for 7–9 hours consistently.
  • 🥩
    Hit your protein targets0.7–1g per pound of bodyweight supports muscle repair and growth.
  • 📈
    Progressive overload is the keyAdd a little more weight, reps, or time each week. Small gains compound massively.
  • 🧊
    Don't skip your warm-up10 minutes of dynamic stretching reduces injury risk and improves performance.
