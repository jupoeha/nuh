Design Principles

01
Edit Ruthlessly
Every object in your home should earn its place. If you don't love it or use it, let it go. Empty space is not wasted space.
02
Layer Your Lighting
Overhead, ambient, task, and accent. The right light at the right moment transforms any room from functional to atmospheric.
03
Bring Nature In
Plants, natural materials, and daylight connect us to the outside world and reduce stress in measurable ways.
04
Invest in the Every Day
Spend more on what you touch constantly — your bed sheets, sofa, kitchen tools. These pay back in comfort every single day.
05
Limit Your Palette
Three colors. Four at most. A limited palette creates cohesion across every room without feeling monotonous.
06
Mix Textures, Not Patterns
Linen, wood, ceramic, wool. Texture adds richness without visual chaos. Let surfaces be your decoration.
