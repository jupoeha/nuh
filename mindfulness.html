Meditation for Beginners
Start with just 5 minutes. Sit comfortably, follow your breath, and gently return when the mind wanders.
⏱ 5–20 min · Daily
📓
Morning Journaling
Three pages of stream-of-consciousness writing each morning clears mental clutter and sharpens focus for the day.
⏱ 15 min · Morning
🌬️
Box Breathing
Inhale 4 counts, hold 4, exhale 4, hold 4. Repeat four times to calm your nervous system in minutes.
⏱ 4 min · Anytime
🚶
Mindful Walking
Leave your phone behind. Notice five things you see, four you can touch, three you can hear, two you can smell.
⏱ 20 min · Outdoors
🙏
Gratitude Practice
Write three specific things you're grateful for each evening. The more specific, the more powerful the effect.
⏱ 5 min · Evening
🌿
Digital Detox Hour
One hour every evening without screens. Read, stretch, cook, or simply sit. Your sleep will thank you.
⏱ 60 min · Evening

Journal Prompts

Writing is thinking made visible. These prompts help you explore what matters most to you right now.

"What would I do today if I knew I couldn't fail?"
"What am I holding onto that I should gently let go?"
"When did I last feel completely at peace? What was different about that moment?"
"What does my ideal ordinary Tuesday look like?"
"Who in my life deserves more of my attention and gratitude?"

The Sleep Ritual

9:00 PM
📱
Screens Off
Put the phone down. Blue light disrupts melatonin for 2 hours.
9:15 PM
🛁
Warm Bath
A 10-minute warm bath drops core body temperature, signaling sleep.
9:45 PM
📖
Read Fiction
30 minutes of light reading relaxes the analytical mind.
10:30 PM
🌙
Lights Out
Cool, dark room (65–68°F). Same time every night. That's the secret.
